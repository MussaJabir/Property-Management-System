What a landlord can do today

  • Sign in to their own private workspace
  • Customise their workspace name, logo, and brand colour
  • Add staff members with the right role (Owner, Manager, Accountant, or Maintenance)
  • Add properties (buildings, compounds, commercial spaces) with photos
  • Add the units inside each property (rooms, apartments, shops, offices, business frames, warehouses)
  • Set the rent for each unit, in monthly, quarterly, every-6-months, yearly, or any custom cycle
  • See a live dashboard with properties, units, occupancy rate, and this month's money in / billed
  • Register tenants — individual or business — with NIDA and TIN stored securely
  • Sign a lease through a step-by-step wizard (renter → unit → terms → confirm)
  • Download the signed lease as a professional PDF (English / Swahili)
  • Activate, end, or terminate a lease — the full history is kept
  • Create invoices with line items, rent + extra charges, and due dates
  • Issue invoices with unique numbers per landlord, per year
  • Record payments in cash, bank transfer, mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money), cheque, or card
  • Automatically issue and email a receipt to the tenant the moment a payment is recorded
  • Download invoice and receipt PDFs anytime
  • Have late invoices flagged automatically every morning
  • Raise maintenance requests with priority (low / medium / high / urgent) and photos
  • Assign maintenance jobs to staff
  • Track maintenance jobs from start → in progress → completed (with cost)
  • Keep a full history of every maintenance job (who did what, when, and any notes)
  • Record property expenses by category (Repair, Cleaning, Security, Utilities, Tax, Other)
  • Attach a photo of the receipt to each expense
  • Filter expenses by category, property, or month
  • Switch the language between English and Swahili anywhere
  • Use it on a phone, tablet, or desktop browser

How we keep it safe and reliable

Each landlord is in their own private space
No landlord can ever see another landlord's tenants, invoices, payments, or expenses. This separation is automatically verified by built-in safety checks.
60 automatic safety tests
The system re-tests itself before every update. If anything important breaks, the update is blocked.
Sensitive data is encrypted
Tenant ID numbers (NIDA and TIN) are stored in encrypted form. Even with direct database access, the raw numbers can't be read.
Money handled exactly
All money is calculated to the shilling. There is no rounding drift, even on very large amounts or unusual billing cycles.
Invoice numbers never collide
Even if multiple staff members issue invoices at the exact same moment, every invoice gets a unique sequential number per landlord.
Built for English & Swahili from day one
Every label and PDF template supports both languages. Users can switch anywhere with one click.
Files stored on professional cloud storage
Property photos, lease PDFs, and receipts are stored on a dedicated cloud storage service — never on the application server itself.
Works on phone, tablet & desktop
The whole landlord interface adapts to whatever screen the staff member is using. No separate mobile app needed at this stage.

What's coming next

Stage 8 — Reports & Analytics is queued to start immediately. This adds the financial and operational reports that landlords need for monthly reviews: rent collection, outstanding rent, occupancy, property income, expense, and profit. Every report is downloadable as both a PDF (for printing or sharing) and an Excel file (for further analysis).

After that, the tenant self-service portal (Stage 9) and the per-landlord public website (Stage 10) round out the tenant-facing side. The final two stages handle polish and the move to the live server.

The integrations being added later (Version 2)

The system is built with hooks for the following features, which we'll switch on after the first version is in production:

  • Mobile money payments — M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money via Selcom (currently recorded manually)
  • SMS reminders — rent due, overdue, payment confirmation via Beem Africa
  • WhatsApp receipts — send receipts directly to the tenant on WhatsApp
  • Login by phone OTP — tenants log in with their phone number and a one-time code
  • Automatic invoice generation — the system creates next month's invoices on its own
  • Live updates — dashboard numbers refresh in real time as payments come in
