Status Report
Date: 4 June 2026
• Live in production

This period focused on running the business side of PMS: getting paid, controlling access, and giving each landlord a tidy place to manage their own account. We also closed an important data-privacy issue and confirmed the fix with an automatic test.

BJP can now suspend a non-paying client (which instantly cuts off their access and notifies them), record subscription payments in one place, and reactivate clients in a click. Landlords get a single, organised Settings screen, and the BJP control panel now opens to a real overview.

What we completed in this period

1. Data-privacy fix — each landlord sees only their own data
We found and fixed an issue where a landlord's staff panel could display another landlord's records. Each workspace is now strictly separated, and an automatic test now guards this so it can never silently return.
2. Suspend & reactivate clients (with notifications)
Suspending a client previously did nothing. Now it actually takes effect:
  • The client's staff panel and public website are immediately blocked, showing a polite "temporarily unavailable" page.
  • The client owner automatically receives an email when their workspace is suspended — and again when it is reactivated.
  • This is the enforcement behind billing: stop paying → suspended; pay → reactivated.
3. Subscription billing — a place to track what clients pay us
A new Subscriptions area in the BJP control panel shows every client's plan, status, renewal date (overdue ones flagged), and total paid to date.
  • A "Record payment" button logs each payment (amount, date, method, reference) and automatically extends the client's paid period and reactivates them.
  • Every payment is kept on record per client. Manual for now; a self-service online-payment option can follow later.
4. Email delivery & branded welcome email
The platform now sends email through the company's own no-reply@bjptechnologies.co.tz address (straight to the inbox). The new-client welcome email was redesigned with proper PMS branding — a clean header, a clear sign-in details card, and a one-click button.
5. A single, organised Settings screen for landlords
Landlord settings used to be scattered. They are now in one place with clear tabs:
  • Business (name, contact), Branding (logo, colour), Preferences (language).
  • Security — change your own password here.
  • Subscription — see your current plan, status and renewal date at a glance.
6. BJP control-panel overview & listing polish
The BJP super-admin panel now opens to a real dashboard (total clients, active, on trial, units across the platform, clients-by-plan, recent signups). On the landlord side, unit lists now show a single clean cover photo per unit instead of every image at once.

How we keep it safe & reliable

  • Strict separation between landlords' data
  • Automatic tests guarding that separation
  • 107 automatic tests run before each update
  • Suspension cuts off non-paying clients
  • Email signed by the company domain
  • HTTPS, nightly backups, encrypted IDs
  • Every change reviewed before going live
  • Bilingual throughout (English & Kiswahili)

What's next

With billing, access control and account settings in place, the next focus remains the Version 2 integrations already prepared in the system: mobile-money payments (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money), SMS and WhatsApp reminders, phone-number login for renters, and automatic monthly invoice generation. An optional online self-service payment flow for client subscriptions can also be scheduled.
