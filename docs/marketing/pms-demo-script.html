Demo Script How to show PMS to a landlord
Before you start: open the demo workspace and sign in as the landlord beforehand, so you begin on the dashboard. Keep it under 10 minutes. Let the prospect hold the phone if they want. Talk about their problem, not the software. Goal of the demo isn’t to explain every button — it’s to make them say “I want this.”

The walkthrough

1
Show the dashboard. Point to the summary cards.
“This is your control center. At a glance: how many properties and units you have, how many are occupied, the rent expected this month, and how much you’ve already collected.”
2
Open Properties & Units. Show occupied vs vacant.
“All your buildings and units in one place — homes, business frames, shops, warehouses. You can see instantly which are occupied and which are empty.”
3
Open a tenant’s lease, download the contract PDF.
“Every tenant has a full record. And with one click, the system gives you a professional lease contract as a PDF — ready to print and sign. No more typing contracts by hand.”
4
Record a payment, then download the receipt PDF.
“When a tenant pays — cash, bank or mobile money — you record it here, and the system instantly produces a numbered receipt you can print or send to them. Every shilling tracked.”
5
Open the Outstanding Rent view.
“Here’s everyone who still owes you, and exactly how much. No more guessing or flipping through a notebook — you know in seconds who to follow up.”
6
Open Maintenance. Show a request assigned to staff.
“A tenant reported a leaking tap. You assign it to your fundi and track it until it’s done — nothing gets forgotten.”
7
Open Reports, export one to PDF.
“At month-end, one click gives you rent collection, occupancy, and profit per property — as PDF or Excel, for your own records or the bank.”
8
Show the tenant portal & the public website.
“Your tenants get their own login to check bills and pay history. And you get a web page that advertises your empty units automatically — all in Swahili or English, on any phone.”
Close strong
  • End with a question: “Shall we set up your properties this week so you can try it with your real tenants?”
  • Ask what they use now and what frustrates them most — listen more than you talk.
  • Note which “coming soon” feature excites them (WhatsApp reminders, mobile-money payments) — that tells us what to build next.

Mwongozo wa Onyesho Jinsi ya kumwonyesha mwenye nyumba PMS
Kabla ya kuanza: fungua eneo la onyesho na uingie kama mwenye nyumba mapema, ili uanze kwenye dashibodi. Tumia chini ya dakika 10. Mruhusu mteja ashike simu kama anataka. Zungumza kuhusu tatizo lake, si programu. Lengo si kueleza kila kitufe — ni kumfanya aseme “Hii naitaka.”

Hatua za onyesho

1
Onyesha dashibodi. Onyesha kadi za muhtasari.
“Hapa ndio kituo chako cha kuongoza. Kwa mtazamo mmoja: una majengo na vyumba vingapi, vingapi vimepangwa, kodi inayotarajiwa mwezi huu, na umeshakusanya kiasi gani.”
2
Fungua Majengo & Vyumba. Onyesha vilivyopangwa na vilivyo wazi.
“Majengo na vyumba vyako vyote sehemu moja — nyumba, vyumba vya biashara, maduka, maghala. Unaona papo hapo kipi kimepangwa na kipi ki wazi.”
3
Fungua mkataba wa mpangaji, pakua PDF ya mkataba.
“Kila mpangaji ana kumbukumbu kamili. Na kwa mbofyo mmoja, mfumo unakupa mkataba wa kitaalamu kama PDF — tayari kuchapa na kusaini. Hakuna tena kuandika mikataba kwa mkono.”
4
Rekodi malipo, kisha pakua PDF ya risiti.
“Mpangaji akilipa — cash, benki au simu — unarekodi hapa, na mfumo unatengeneza risiti yenye namba papo hapo unayoweza kuchapa au kumtumia. Kila shilingi inafuatiliwa.”
5
Fungua orodha ya Wanaodaiwa Kodi.
“Hapa ni kila anayekudai, na kiasi kamili. Hakuna tena kubahatisha au kupekua daftari — unajua sekunde chache nani wa kumfuatilia.”
6
Fungua Matengenezo. Onyesha ombi lililopewa mfanyakazi.
“Mpangaji ameripoti bomba linalovuja. Unampa fundi wako na unalifuatilia mpaka limekamilika — hakuna linalosahaulika.”
7
Fungua Ripoti, hamisha moja kwa PDF.
“Mwisho wa mwezi, mbofyo mmoja unakupa ukusanyaji wa kodi, ujazo, na faida ya kila jengo — kama PDF au Excel, kwa kumbukumbu zako au benki.”
8
Onyesha tovuti ya mpangaji & tovuti ya umma.
“Wapangaji wako wanapata kuingia kwao kuangalia bili na historia ya malipo. Na wewe unapata ukurasa wa wavuti unaotangaza vyumba vyako vilivyo wazi kiotomatiki — yote kwa Kiswahili au Kiingereza, kwa simu yoyote.”
Funga kwa nguvu
  • Maliza kwa swali: “Tukuwekee majengo yako wiki hii ili ujaribu na wapangaji wako halisi?”
  • Uliza anatumia nini sasa na kinachomuudhi zaidi — sikiliza zaidi kuliko kuongea.
  • Angalia kipengele cha “kinakuja hivi karibuni” kinachomvutia (vikumbusho vya WhatsApp, malipo ya simu) — hicho kinatuonyesha tujenge nini baadaye.
